PORTFOLIO

Projects

In 2012, I moved to Washington, DC, and began to work as a graphic designer for a startup called TechChange, a social enterprise that provides online courses on the use of technology. This opportunity opened the door for me to work with the United States Institute of Peace: Global Campus1, a project that leverages the latest modern communication technologies to extend online education and training opportunities to individuals across the world. In this position I served as the lead designer, working in close collaboration with content experts, web developers, motion graphics teams, videographers and instructional designers.

The work I've done with the USIP Global Campus includes the design of print and web material, iconography, infographics, and the development of online simulations. I have also helped develop an original online learning platform2, which offers a suite of online courses in the field of peacebuilding.
